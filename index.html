    Project Idea

    Overview:

      My project idea is to create a Travel-planning app that will arrange and create an ultimate trip without using many apps at the same time depending on the user’s personality and characteristics. The users only need to fill all the information requested and The Travel-planning app will find and show a lot of different packages which contain all of the information and activities which the travelers can just follow it as an online tour guide for users to choose.

    Motivation:

      There are a lot of different travel apps which occurred and were more popular in recent years but these other apps still miss some point or just focus on only 1 aspect such as accommodations, food and travelers have to use many different apps or rely on other services to have a nice vacation. However, this project appears to be able to solve the problem which helps people vacation better with a nice deal and reduce many unrelated costs as well as create more inspiration for others to travel to many different places, thereby developing the country's tourism industry.

    Description:

      Currently, traveling has not only become a hobby but also turned into a lifestyle for many people around the world. There are more and more jobs that tend to rely on traveling to be able to develop better such as Travel Bloggers, Youtuber, going abroad, people on vacations, those who travel for business or get experiences, knowledge. Therefore, the Travel-planning apps were created to improve the quality of people’s vacation, trip and make us easier to adapt in a strange place just using a smartphone which always stays with us wherever we go as our second self. People nowadays usually use smartphones to search information, book and buy everything online and it will be the same while traveling so the Travel-planning app is going to be the great app for people instead of spending a long time to organize or using lots of different kinds of apps to plan your trip.

      There is just a little requirement for users to run the app. Firstly, the users should be able to fill out some of the information required, it is not going to be too private information but it's just a few things which relate to the trip which you are going to enjoy. For example, your budget, hobbies, favorite scenery, activities to do or some specifics which attract to do during the vacation … and everyone will have different results depending on their differences about personalities and hobbies. Secondly, From that kind of information, the app will figure out and show the list of packages of different locations which contain all of the necessary information for users and it is not only just about some of the simple things such as some good hotels, delicious restaurant, activities to do … but also contain the specific and details information like how’s the weather of that day, the service of the hotel, restaurant with customers rating, a good time to go out, to eat, to do these kinds of activities which has been launched before but it will cost a little money for that. When people can definitely choose the location which they want to travel, in every package of each place, there are a lot of different options for users to choose or compare each other and find the most suitable option for themself.
